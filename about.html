About

QueerCountryAZ was founded by resident artist Bryn Parker of These Loud thoughts, a Tucson musician who relocated to phoenix during the pandemic. They noticed a lack of community for queer country and folk artists in the sprawling cityscape. To address this issue, they began utilizing their own gigs to develop a stronger sense of community amongst artists and the audience. In the summer of 2023, the first Queer Country Night was hosted at The Rhythm Room. The turnout was so positive that they knew they had something special. This website is the beginning stages of what hopes to be a thriving community throughout Arizona that focuses on platforming local, queer, country and folk artists.
